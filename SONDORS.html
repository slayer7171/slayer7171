A comprehenisive review on the Sondors Fold X




This is the EBike that I currently own and ride on a daily basis. The Sondors Fold X is ground breaking when it comes to heavy duty E-bikes. The bike has a range of anywhere from 60 to 90 miles with a top speed of 20mph. The bikes battery is very discreet and can be removed easily. There is a 7 speed gear shift to allow for easy to more strenuous pedaling. The bike out performs all other heavy duty ebikes on the market for even less money With the 300 pound load capacity, basically anyone can ride it with ease.

PROS

  • Range 40-60 miles
  • Speed 20 MPH
  • Built like a tank
  • Fat Tires for the beach
  • Beautiful Paint Job

CONS

  • Bulky/heavy
  • Could be dangerous
  • Charge time
  • Takes a very long time to receive
