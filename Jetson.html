Jetson Metro

The Jetson metro is a great afforable E-bike that offers a lot of usuability in a city type atmosphere. The Metro can easily be packed up into the back of a car or taken on a train. The battery lasts a pretty long time and the bike needs very little Maintenance.

PROS

  • Range 25 miles
  • Speed 15.5 MPH
  • Pretty light
  • Nice LCD Display
  • 3 Electric Speeds
  • Can fit basically anywhere

CONS

  • Load Capacity
  • Not the best customer support
  • Only one gear
  • Only comes in white and black
